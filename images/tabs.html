Sustaining Generosity: Building a Legacy of
Sadaqah Jariyah and Community Welfare

Discover our mission to create a legacy of lasting charity through Sadaqah Jariyah. We cover burial expenses, facilitate Hajj and Umrah journeys, and invest in sustainable charitable ventures. Join us in building a brighter future for all.

Sadaqah Jariyah

Envision yourself being a part of an ever-growing Sadaqah Jariyah. Our concept revolves around offering Sadaqah Jariyah on behalf of all members past and present, ensuring its perpetuity for future generations until Yawm Al Qiyamah (the Day of Resurrection).

Burial fund

We strive to cover the burial expenses of our members whenever possible, prioritizing our most vulnerable individuals, particularly revert Muslims lacking support or a Muslim family.

Hajj and Umrah fund

Each year, we will endeavor to facilitate the journey of as many individuals as possible to Hajj and Umrah, subject to availability. Sending each person will serve as a sadaqah on behalf of all contributing members

Waqf fund

The Waqf fund has two primary objectives. One is to leverage talent to benefit Muslims whenever possible. The other is to establish profitable business opportunities, with the generated profits being directed towards charitable causes.
